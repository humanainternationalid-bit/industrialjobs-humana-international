Assistant Manager Process Safety

North Sumatra, Indonesia
Full-time

About Our Client: Our client is a leading global manufacturing company committed to the highest standards of Safety, Health, and Environment (SHE). They are looking for an experienced and passionate professional to join their dynamic team.

Job Purpose: To support the SHE Site Delivery Manager in managing and enhancing the overall Process Safety Management (PSM) system, ensuring compliance with international and local standards, and fostering a proactive safety culture across our client's manufacturing plants.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and review Process Safety procedures and work instructions based on global standards and industry best practices.
  • Lead and facilitate periodic process risk assessments and workshops such as HAZOP, Bowtie, and What-If.
  • Schedule and participate in internal and external Process Safety audits.
  • Ensure all process-related incidents and near-misses are reported and thoroughly investigated.
  • Act as a key reviewer for all Management of Change (MOC) requests.
  • Initiate reviews of emergency response procedures and ensure the execution of yearly drill schedules.
  • Define, manage, and audit safety critical equipment (SCE).
  • Participate in Pre-Startup Safety Reviews (PSSR) for new equipment or projects.

Qualifications & Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Engineering (Chemical, Mechanical, Industrial, or related field).
  • Minimum of 10 years of experience in Process Safety within Oleochemical, Petrochemical, Oil & Gas, or Chemical industries.
  • Proficient in international Process Safety standards (NFPA, API, ANSI) and local regulations.
  • Hands-on experience with HAZOP, QRA, Bowtie Analysis, and PHA.
  • Familiar with Life Critical Standards, MOC, and SCE.

Environmental Assistant Manager

North Sumatra, Indonesia
Full-time

About Our Client: On behalf of our client, a leading multinational manufacturing company in the oleochemical industry, we are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Environmental Assistant Manager.

This is a key role focused on driving environmental excellence, ensuring regulatory compliance, and supporting the company's sustainability goals at their state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. You will report directly to the SHE Manager and play a crucial part in maintaining a safe and environmentally responsible workplace.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Compliance & Standards: Ensure and maintain full compliance with all environmental standards, government regulations (e.g., PROPER), and corporate policies.
  • Policy & Procedure: Develop, implement, and continuously improve environmental procedures, guidelines, and management systems for all plant operations.
  • Risk Management: Initiate and lead environmental risk management activities, including Job Safety Analysis (JSA) and Environmental Aspect & Impact Assessments.
  • Auditing: Manage and coordinate annual internal audits for critical safety and environmental standards.
  • Reporting: Oversee and ensure the timely and accurate submission of mandatory government reports, including RKL-RPL and EPR documentation.
  • Incident Prevention: Actively support management in the prevention of environmental incidents, near misses, and releases.
  • Stakeholder Collaboration: Lead and coordinate with cross-functional teams to plan and execute strategic environmental programs.
  • Management Systems: Drive the review and improvement of the Integrated Management System (IMS), specifically focusing on the ISO 14001 framework.

Required Qualifications & Experience:

  • Bachelor's Degree (S1) in Environmental Engineering or a related technical field.
  • A minimum of 10 years of experience in Safety, Health & Environment (SHE), with at least 5 years in a leadership role.
  • Mandatory experience in the oleochemical, chemical, or oil & gas downstream industry.
  • In-depth knowledge of oleochemical products handling.
  • Proficient in implementation and management of ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems.
  • Proven experience in managing RKL-RPL and EPR reporting in accordance with Indonesian regulations.
  • Strong leadership, analytical, communication, and cross-functional coordination skills.
  • Fluent in English (verbal and written).

  • Exceptional communication, negotiation, and presentation skills, with the ability to engage effectively with diverse stakeholders, from local communities to ministerial levels.
  • Demonstrated ability to analyze complex socio-political issues, solve problems under pressure, and make wise decisions that balance external demands with corporate interests.

Project Manager

Pasuruan, East Java, Indonesia
Full-time

About Our Client: Our client is a globally recognized manufacturing leader with a distinguished 250-year legacy. Consistently acclaimed as one of the "Worlds Best Workplaces," the company specializes in pioneering sustainable and innovative industrial solutions, with a significant presence across more than 50 countries. They are deeply committed to operational excellence, diversity, and fostering an industry-leading safety culture.

Role Purpose:

  • The Project Manager will be entrusted with the end-to-end leadership and delivery of a critical new production facility. This high-impact role involves full accountability for the construction of a new building, installation of state-of-the-art machinery, and ensuring the project is launched successfully into operations. The successful candidate will be the key driver in ensuring the project is completed on time, within budget, to the highest quality and safety standards.

Key Responsibilities:

  • End-to-End Project Leadership: Take full ownership of the project lifecycle, from planning and execution to handover, for a new dyeing and finishing production building.
  • Construction Management: Oversee all civil, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing), and utilities construction, ensuring strict adherence to quality procedures, technical drawings, and specifications.
  • Machinery Installation & Commissioning: Plan and execute the installation of dyeing, finishing, and utility machines, ensuring operational readiness and identifying opportunities for process optimization.
  • Stakeholder Coordination: Act as the central point of coordination between consultants, contractors, internal cross-functional teams, and local authorities to ensure seamless project execution.
  • HSE & Compliance Stewardship: Champion a zero-incident culture by rigorously implementing and enforcing all health, safety, and environmental (HSE) policies. Ensure full compliance with all local regulatory and permit requirements.
  • Budget & Timeline Management: Maintain meticulous project documentation, track progress against milestones, manage the budget, and proactively identify and mitigate risks to avoid delays and cost overruns.

Qualifications & Experience:

  • A Bachelors degree in Electrical or Mechanical Engineering is required.
  • A minimum of 5-7 years of proven experience in a Construction Manager or Project Manager role, with a track record of managing large-scale, complex construction or industrial projects from start to finish.
  • Essential experience working within a multinational corporation (MNC) in a similar project management capacity.
  • Strong technical expertise in both electrical and mechanical systems, with the ability to read and interpret technical drawings.
  • Fluent professional proficiency in English and Bahasa Indonesia is mandatory.
  • A results-driven and cost-oriented mindset, combined with exceptional problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and team coaching abilities to manage diverse stakeholders and foster a collaborative project environment.
